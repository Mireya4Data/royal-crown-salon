 Royal Crown Salon Booking & Management Platform

 Overview

Royal Crown Salon Booking & Management Platform is a full-stack web application designed to digitize salon operations by providing an efficient appointment booking system, customer management functionality, and administrative oversight tools.

The platform enables customers to browse available services, select preferred staff members, schedule appointments, and manage bookings online. Administrators can monitor appointments, manage customer activity, and track salon operations through a centralized dashboard.



Business Problem

Traditional salon booking methods often rely on phone calls, walk-ins, or messaging platforms, making it difficult to efficiently manage appointments and customer records.

Common challenges include:

* Double bookings
* Missed appointments
* Limited appointment visibility
* Manual customer tracking
* Time-consuming administrative processes

Royal Crown Salon addresses these challenges by automating the booking workflow and providing a centralized management system.
